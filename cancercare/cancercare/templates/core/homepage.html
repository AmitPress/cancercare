Get Educated
The Cancer Education Program (CEP) was established in 1995 to address the education needs of patients with cancer and caregivers. It links the research and patient care endeavors of CancerCare Clinic Cancer Center to provide the best possible experience for each individual who comes through its doors. The institutionally sanctioned and funded CEP within the Cancer Center is unique at CancerCare Clinic. The institutional support demonstrates CancerCare Clinic's recognition of the Cancer Center's role in addressing all aspects of cancer care. The mission of the CEP is to educate all audiences on cancer-related issues, including prevention, risks, treatment, survivorship, and end-of-life care. The CEP offers educational programs, classes and support to patients with cancer, their caregivers, staff and the public at all three CancerCare Clinic sites.
